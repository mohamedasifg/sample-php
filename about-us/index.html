About Us

With five redundant landing stations and four terrestrial networks connected via border crossings, ZOI is positioned to be a global connectivity hub.

We provide the lowest latency network, ensuring optimal network infrastructure for our partners’ applications and services. We make it easier for our partners to deliver services worldwide with superior quality, reliability, and performance.

Our mission

ZOI’s mission is to connect our partners to the world by constantly pushing boundaries.

As the first and only truly pan-regional network, ZOI is synonymous with connection.

We’re strategically located to become the telecom gateway to both the east and west.

We do things differently, boldly,and creatively.

Our vision

The preferred telecom powerhouse earning the trust of partners worldwide.
